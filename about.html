About GasWorld

Est. 1981. Bristol-based. The South West's trusted mechanical heating and plumbing specialists.

Our story

A South West institution since 1981

GasWorld was founded in Bristol in 1981 with a simple principle: deliver exceptional heating and plumbing work, every time. Over four decades later, that principle drives everything we do.

We offer heating, plumbing, and renewable energy services across the South West from our head office in St Annes, Bristol, and our second office in Exeter.

Credentials

MCS certification covers our renewable energy installations a requirement for accessing the Boiler Upgrade Scheme grant.
