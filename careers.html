Opportunities

Roles we're looking for

We recruit across Bristol, Bath, and the wider South West. Whether you're a seasoned engineer or starting an apprenticeship, we'd love to hear from you.

Heating Engineers (Domestic)

Gas Safe registered. Experience in boiler installations, servicing, and repairs across residential properties.

Heating Engineers (Commercial)

Experience in commercial and new build mechanical installations. Used to working on large developments and multi-unit schemes.

Plumbers

Experienced plumbers for domestic and commercial work across the South West. Full callout and project-based work available.

Pipefitters

Skilled pipefitters for commercial new build projects. Experience in distribution pipework, boiler houses, and mechanical services.

Renewables Engineers (ASHP)

MCS-certified or working towards certification. Experience with Air Source Heat Pump installation for GW Renewables.

Apprentices (All Levels)

We welcome apprentices at all stages from school leavers to adult returners. Earn while you learn alongside experienced engineers.

Location: Bristol, Bath, South West and beyond. We operate across the full South West region from our Bristol HQ and Exeter office.
