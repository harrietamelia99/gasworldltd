About GasWorld

Trusted across the South West for over 40 years

GasWorld engineers are highly skilled, experienced, and continually trained across a wide range of heating systems. Based in Bristol since 1981, we've grown into one of the South West's most trusted mechanical heating and plumbing contractors.
