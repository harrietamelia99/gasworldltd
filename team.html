Leadership

Meet the directors

Adam Lane
Managing Director

Adam has steered GasWorld through a period of significant growth, building on the strong foundations laid since 1981. His focus on long-term client relationships, quality of work, and team development has defined GasWorld's reputation across the South West. Adam is actively involved in day-to-day operations and leads the company's strategic direction.

Mike Stephens
Head of Production

Mike oversees all engineering operations and project delivery across GasWorld's commercial, social housing, and domestic divisions. With decades of hands-on experience, he ensures every project from a single boiler replacement to a 200-unit new build is delivered on time, on budget, and to the highest standard.
